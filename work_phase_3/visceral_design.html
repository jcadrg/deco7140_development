Visceral Design

The focus of this section of the Style Guide is ensuring the user has a strong emotional connection at first glance. This includes using color, imagery, layout, and typography to spark immediate engagement and interest.

Our approach is inspired by Don Norman‚Äôs Emotional Design theory. This page explains how we translated that theory into interface decisions for HobbyHub, particularly for the homepage and trending areas.

Page Layouts

Colour Scheme

Our colour scheme was chosen to evoke warmth, energy, and approachability, aligning with CCI‚Äôs goal for a welcoming and inclusive platform. The palette uses soft contrasts for accessibility and vibrant highlights to draw user attention without overwhelming.

Primary

#4B3F72

Used for titles, accents, and key UI highlights. Conveys reliability and focus. Important for high-contrast accessibility.

Accent

#F18F01

Brings playful energy to CTAs, links, and hover effects. Supports user's need for visual encouragement and delight.

Muted

#EDEDED

Used for card backgrounds and soft borders. Keeps the interface clean and low-pressure for casual users.

Surface

#FFFFFF

Base background for most UI components. Balances clarity and neutrality, aiding content visibility and legibility.

Dark

#2E2E2E

Used for headings and body text. Ensures accessibility and readability across all devices and lighting conditions.

Typography

Our typography choices focus on readability, clarity, and emotional resonance. They align with the platform's need to feel both professional and inviting, supporting a range of users from casual hobbyists to dedicated community contributors.

Heading XL

Font: Nunito, 48px, Extra Bold

Used for hero titles, homepage banners, and major CTAs. Captures immediate attention.

Heading Large

Font: Nunito, 36px, Bold

Used for section titles and key UI prompts. Balances visibility and information hierarchy.

Heading Medium

Font: Nunito, 28px, Semi-Bold

Used for sub-sections and component titles. Guides the eye without overwhelming.

Body Large

Font: Nunito, 20px, Regular

Used for descriptions, intros, and platform guidance text. Supports scannability.

Body Medium

Font: Nunito, 16px, Regular

Default paragraph text. Optimized for readability across devices.

Caption

Font: Nunito, 12px, Medium

Used for meta labels, image alt descriptions, and low-emphasis info.
