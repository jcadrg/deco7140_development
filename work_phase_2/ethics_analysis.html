Ethical Considerations

A breakdown of ethical concerns, considerations, and principles applied during the design process.

Informed Consent

  • Participants are fully informed about the purpose and scope of the project.
  • All users involved in interviews or usability testing provided informed consent voluntarily.
  • We ensured participants could opt-out at any time without consequence.

Privacy & Anonymity

  • User identities are anonymized in all public-facing materials.
  • No identifying personal data is stored beyond the purpose of analysis.
  • Secure storage practices were implemented to safeguard digital notes and transcripts.

Inclusive and Ethical Design

  • The design avoids stereotypes and embraces cultural sensitivity.
  • We considered users with a range of abilities, backgrounds, and technological access.
  • Accessibility features follow WCAG 2.1 standards (contrast, keyboard nav, etc.).

Data Ownership

  • All content submitted by users remains their intellectual property.
  • Users are given full control to modify or remove their contributions.
  • Clear guidelines were drafted for how data will be used and stored.
